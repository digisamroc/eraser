How to track broker responses
  1. 1 Send removal requests from the Brokers page
  2. 2 Wait for responses (usually 1-7 days)
  3. 3 Click "Scan Inbox" to fetch broker emails and extract opt-out form links (emails are moved to an "Eraser" folder)
  4. 4 Check "Action Required" below for forms that need manual completion

Tip: If form links aren't appearing, click "Reclassify" to re-analyze emails with the improved extractor.

{{end}} {{if not .InboxConfigured}}

Inbox Monitoring Not Configured

To automatically track broker responses, configure inbox monitoring in your settings.

Add the following to your config.yaml:

inbox:
  enabled: true
  provider: gmail
  email: your-email@gmail.com
  password: your-app-password
